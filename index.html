Madhwa Interpretation

Madhvacharya's Bhagavata Tatparya Nirnaya reveals the Dvaita purport: every lila of Krishna demonstrates His supreme independence (Swatantrya). The Gopis' love represents the highest Ananya Bhakti. Gajendra's surrender is the model of Prapatti (Sharanagati). Prahlada teaches us that devotion cannot be destroyed by any external force. The Bhagavatam's core teaching: Hari Sarvottama — Vishnu alone is the Supreme.
