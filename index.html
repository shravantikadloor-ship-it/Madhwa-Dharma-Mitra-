📖 The 12 Skandhas
Madhwa Interpretation

Madhvacharya's Bhagavata Tatparya Nirnaya reveals the Dvaita purport: every lila of Krishna demonstrates His supreme independence (Swatantrya). The Gopis' love represents the highest Ananya Bhakti. Gajendra's surrender (Prapatti) is the supreme model. Prahlada teaches that devotion cannot be destroyed. Ajamila shows that even an inadvertent utterance of the Lord's name grants liberation. Core message: Hari Sarvottama — Vishnu alone is the Supreme.
